# Electrical Contractor Training Market — WAI Institute

## Market Opportunity

**Target:** Licensed electricians and electrical contractors (LCE — Lightning City Electric is the model)  
**Market Size:** ~300,000 licensed electricians in North America  
**Pain Point:** Compliance training, apprentice onboarding, skill certification are expensive, fragmented  
**Solution:** WAI's 9-lab hands-on training system + team licensing + NEC/NFPA compliance guides

---

## Revenue Tiers

### Solo Contractor ($29/mo)
- 1 seat, unlimited lab access
- Voltage drop calculator, conduit bending calculator
- NFPA 70E quick-ref, NEC section guides
- **Ideal for:** Sole proprietors, independent electricians

### Small Crew ($99/mo)
- 5 seats (add more at $15/seat)
- Team dashboard, completion tracking
- Bulk cert export (for apprentice portfolios)
- Admin controls
- **Ideal for:** 2-5 person shops

### Growing Crew ($249/mo)
- 20 seats (add more at $10/seat)
- Advanced analytics & reporting
- Custom team branding (logo on certs)
- Priority support + quarterly webinars
- **Ideal for:** 10-50 person companies

### Enterprise (Custom)
- Unlimited seats, custom licensing terms
- White-label option (contractor's own branding)
- API access for custom integrations
- Dedicated account manager
- **Ideal for:** Electrical unions, trade schools, large companies

## Lab Offerings (9 modules, all hands-on)

1. **Basic Circuit Builder** (1 hr) — Ohm's Law, series/parallel networks
2. **Switch & Receptacle Wiring** (1 hr) — 3-way switches, NFPA compliance
3. **Breaker Panel Labeling** (1 hr) — Load balancing, NEC 408.4
4. **Conduit Bending Calculator** (1 hr) — Offsets, saddles, shrink calculations
5. **Voltage Drop Calculator** (1 hr) — Conductor sizing, NEC formula
6. **Safety & PPE Fundamentals** (1 hr) — NFPA 70E, LOTO, hazard recognition
7. **Tools & Equipment Inspection** (1 hr) — Meter usage, tool maintenance
8. **Troubleshooting Logic** (1 hr) — Fault isolation, continuity testing
9. **Solar & Off-Grid Systems** (1 hr) — PV sizing, batteries, charge controllers

**Certifications:** Each lab completion gets a timestamped certificate. Contractors can export bulk certs for apprentice portfolios or LinkedIn.
