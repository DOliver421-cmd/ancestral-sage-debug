# Customer Support SLA — Response & Resolution Times

**For:** WAI-Institute & M.O.R.E. Help Center  
**Effective:** 2026-05-22  
**Owner:** Delon Oliver

---

## SUPPORT CHANNELS & SLA

### TIER 1: TYPICAL ISSUES (Session-Based)
**What:** Normal questions, course access, billing clarifications, technical help

**Response Time:** Same-day (within 4 hours during business hours)  
**Resolution Time:** 24-48 hours  
**Channel:** In-app chat + email  
**Handled By:** Support staff (escalate to Delon if complex)

**Examples:**
- "I can't access Module 3"
- "How do I download my certificate?"
- "What's the difference between BASIC and ADVANCED?"
- "How do I reset my password?"

---

### TIER 2: COMPLEX ISSUES (Email-Based)
**What:** Refund requests, billing disputes, account issues, feature requests, partnerships

**Response Time:** 24 hours (acknowledge receipt + timeline)  
**Resolution Time:** 3-5 business days  
**Channel:** Email (priority-flagged)  
**Handled By:** Delon (with Claude support for analysis)

**Examples:**
- "I want to request a refund"
- "My payment failed and I was charged twice"
- "Can you customize the curriculum for our company?"
- "I have a partnership proposal"
- "I found a bug in the system"

---

### TIER 3: LOW-PRIORITY / ADMINISTRATIVE (Email)
**What:** General inquiries, feedback, suggestions, non-urgent feature requests that don't impact revenue or compliance

**Response Time:** 36 hours (or as legally required, whichever is sooner)  
**Resolution Time:** Best effort (may be deferred or declined)  
**Channel:** Email (standard priority)  
**Handled By:** Support staff (Delon reviews weekly)

**Examples:**
- "Can you add a dark mode?"
- "What's your company mission?"
- "I have a suggestion for the UI"
- "Can I get a bulk discount?"
- "Do you have an API?"

**Note:** These are good-to-have, not critical. Response time may extend if higher-priority issues require attention. If no response within 36 hours, user can escalate to Tier 2.

---

## ESCALATION PATH

```
User Issue
    ↓
Support Staff (Tier 1 response)
    ↓
Can resolve? → YES → Close with solution
    ↓
NO → Escalate to Delon (Tier 2 email)
    ↓
Can Delon resolve? → YES → Close
    ↓
NO → Escalate to Claude (technical/complex)
    ↓
Claude provides analysis/solution
    ↓
Delon decides + communicates final answer
```

---

## RESPONSE TIME MEASUREMENT

**"Response Time" means:**
- Tier 1: First human acknowledges issue (may not be full resolution)
- Tier 2: Delon/Claude reads and starts working on it
- Tier 3: Support staff reads (may not act immediately)

**"Resolution Time" means:**
- Issue is fully resolved and customer is notified

---

## EXCEPTIONS

**Issues That Bypass SLA (Immediate Priority):**
- Security breach or data exposure
- System down (502 errors, payment processing broken)
- Legal issue or regulatory requirement
- Customer at risk of harm
- Competitor/partnership opportunity

**Response:** Immediate (within 1 hour)  
**Handled By:** Delon (with Claude emergency support)

## REFUND HANDLING (High Priority)

Refund requests are **Tier 2** (Email) by default:

**Process:**
1. Customer emails: "I want a refund"
2. Support staff acknowledges within 2 hours: "We'll process this"
3. **7-day unconditional refund:** Automatic (no questions)
4. **After 7 days:** Delon reviews + decides (case-by-case)
5. **Refund issued:** Within 5 business days to original payment method

**Note:** All refund requests are logged for metrics + compliance.
