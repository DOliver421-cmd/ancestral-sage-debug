## HOW PREMIUM SUPPORT WORKS

### Student Subscription ($99/month includes)
- 1 monthly 30-minute 1-on-1 call with assigned mentor
- Email support (replies within 48 hours)
- Resume review (once per 3 months)
- Interview prep (mock interviews on request)
- Access to job board (exclusive opportunities)
- Career resources (salary data, company reviews, negotiation tips)

### Booking Your Mentor
```
1. Choose mentor from roster (based on your needs/schedule)
2. Pick 1 slot per month (30 min call)
3. Optional: Add capstone project mentoring (+$199)
4. Optional: Add job placement guarantee (+$299)
5. Mentor sends call link, you meet 1-on-1
```

### What to Prepare for Your Call
```
Before each call, prepare:
1. What you want to discuss (specific question or topic)
2. What you're stuck on (or celebrating!)
3. One action item you'll do before next call
```

### Between Calls
- Mentors respond to emails within 48 hours
- You can ask questions anytime
- Mentors check their schedule weekly to keep it updated
