## CASE-BY-CASE REFUNDS (After 7 Days)

After the 7-day window, refunds are evaluated individually. Delon reviews based on:

**Likely to be approved:**
- Technical issue prevented course access (site was down, videos didn't load)
- Medical/family emergency requiring pause
- Discovered duplicate charge (charged twice by mistake)
- Course content significantly different from description

**Reviewed but may be declined:**
- "I changed my mind" (outside 7-day window)
- Completed most of the course ("I got what I needed")
- Low personal motivation ("I didn't have time to study")
- Dispute with mentor (mediatable, not refundable)

**Not refundable:**
- Sunk cost ("I already finished Module 3")
- Personal reasons unrelated to service ("I lost my job")
- Attempted to manipulate system (abuse)

**Process:**
1. Email request to `refund@wai-institute.com`
2. Explain your situation (be honest)
3. Delon reviews within 3 business days
4. Email response with decision + reasoning
5. If approved, refund issued within 5 business days

## PARTIAL REFUNDS (Proration)

### Monthly Subscriptions (Mid-Cycle Cancellation)

If you cancel mid-month, you get refunded for unused days.

**Formula:**
```
Days remaining in billing cycle = [Billing end date] - [Cancellation date]
Daily rate = [Monthly price] / [Days in month]
Refund = Days remaining × Daily rate
```

**Example:**
- You pay $29.99 on May 1 (30-day May)
- You cancel on May 11 (20 days used, 10 days remaining)
- Daily rate: $29.99 / 30 = $0.9997 per day
- Refund: $0.9997 × 10 = $9.99

### Annual Subscriptions (Early Cancellation)

If you pay for annual ($299.99) and cancel before 365 days:

**Refund = [Annual price] × [Unused months] / 12**

**Example:**
- You pay $299.99 on May 1 (annual)
- You cancel on August 1 (3 months used, 9 months remaining)
- Refund: $299.99 × (9/12) = $224.99

## REFUND PROCESSING

### Timeline
- **Approval:** 24-48 hours
- **Processing:** 3-5 business days (bank-dependent)
- **Your account:** Access revoked immediately upon request

### Payment Method
Refunds go back to your **original payment method**:
- Credit card → refund to card (your bank shows as credit)
- PayPal → refund to PayPal account
- Bank transfer → refund to bank account

### International Refunds
If you paid in foreign currency:
- Refund issued in original currency
- Exchange rate used is day of refund (not purchase day)
- Bank fees for international transfer may apply

## FAQ

**Q: Can I get a refund if I didn't like the course?**  
A: Within 7 days, yes. After 7 days, only if there was a technical issue or material was misrepresented.

**Q: Can I get a refund if I completed the whole course?**  
A: No. You got what you paid for. We don't refund for satisfaction after completion.

**Q: Can I pause instead of cancel?**  
A: Not yet, but that's on the roadmap. For now, cancel and re-enroll when ready (7-day guarantee still applies).

**Q: How long does the refund take?**  
A: 3-5 business days to your account (varies by bank).

**Q: Do I keep my certificate if I get refunded?**  
A: If you earned it before refund, yes. If you haven't earned it, you lose access.

**Q: Can I get a refund + keep access to the course?**  
A: No. Refund = access revoked immediately.
