Stand in the room. You are Earth. Speak the map into existence.

What You Must Do

Step 01 — Download & Open

Save this file to your device. Open it in any browser — Chrome, Firefox, Safari. No internet required. Everything is inside this single file.

Step 02 — Set Up Your Space

Connect your device to your PA system or speaker. Open this page full-screen (F11 on desktop, or pinch-zoom on mobile). Dim the lights. Let the room settle.

Step 03 — Enter Teleprompter Mode

Press "Teleprompter Mode" below the script. The page goes full black. The incantation fills the screen in large text — readable from a distance. Use it as your scroll behind you while you speak to the room.

Step 04 — Speak the Cosmic Map

Read the incantation aloud into the PA. Click each line as you speak it — it highlights magenta so you never lose your place, and dims when done. Press "Reset" to start over.

Step 05 — Reference the Civilization Cards

The cards below give you each civilization's direction, planet, and trait. Use them to improvise deeper commentary on any civilization mid-performance.

To download: Right-click this page → Save As → save as starbridge-sector.html. Or in Chrome: File → Save Page As. Send the single .html file to anyone — it runs with no server, no app, no install.

Keepers of all timelines. They do not live in the present — they observe from the archive of every moment that has ever been.

+Y · -Gravitic

Kor'taan Swarm

A hive consciousness that rejects gravity as constraint. They build upward without limit. They have never touched the ground.

-Y · +Solar

Luminari Ascendants

Solarion

Children of starfire. They absorb solar energy directly into their consciousness. The closer to a sun, the more aware they become.

+Z · +Organic

Orphic Legion

Vor'Kal

Biological architects. Every structure on Vor'Kal is grown, not built. The planet itself is their co-author.

-Z · +Psychic

Silent Constellation

Somnara

They have not spoken aloud in three thousand years. Every word is transmitted — mind to mind, star to star, dream to dream.
