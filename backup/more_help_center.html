About MORE & WAI-Institute
Who we are, why we exist, and how we're governed.

Our Mission

WAI-Institute (Workforce Apprentice Institute) was founded by D. Oliver to multiply resources, credentials, and empowerment for communities historically excluded from the institutions that train, certify, and hire — with primary service to Black communities and underserved populations.

The platform treats members as stakeholders. Revenue is fuel for the mission. Community accountability is structural, not aspirational.

⚖

Governance

A Council of 24 Elders holds review authority over platform decisions. AI safety constraints are cryptographically verified.
