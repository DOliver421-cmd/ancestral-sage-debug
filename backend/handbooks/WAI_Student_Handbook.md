## 1. Welcome to WAI

You are now an apprentice at the Workforce Apprentice Institute. WAI exists to give you the skills, credentials, and support to build wealth in your community. This is not a traditional school — it is a launchpad.

---

## 2. The Mission

> "WAI Institute and M.O.R.E. Help Center exist to multiply resources and empowerment for communities that have been locked out of the institutions that build wealth, opportunity, and influence."

You are not just learning a trade. You are reclaiming power for your people.

---

## 3. What You Will Learn

The core program covers **12 modules** (142 total hours) in electrical trades, delivered from a mobile classroom:

- Electrical safety and professional PPE use
- DC and AC circuit fundamentals
- Wiring, switches, and receptacles
- Subpanel installation and load calculations
- Conduit bending and raceway installation
- Grounding and bonding per code
- Off-grid solar PV system design
- Battery bank and inverter wiring
- Full mobile classroom commissioning

---

## 4. How You Will Be Evaluated

WAI uses **competency-based assessment**:

- You advance by demonstrating skills, not by sitting in a seat
- Each module ends with a practical task you must complete
- Written quizzes confirm theory understanding
- The final exam is commissioning a live system

There are no grades. There is only **competent** or **not yet competent**.

---

## 5. M.O.R.E. Help Center

You have access to the Michael Oliver Resource Exchange:

- Housing assistance
- Legal help

- Health and wellness

Talk to your instructor or visit the M.O.R.E. Hub.

## 6. Student Responsibilities

- Arrive on time and prepared (PPE, notebook, tools)
- Follow all safety protocols — no exceptions
- Respect instructors, peers, and equipment
- Complete competency checklists before advancing
- Report unsafe conditions immediately

---

## 7. Your Path Forward

After completing the core program, you can:

- Enroll in advanced certification tracks
- Create and sell courses on the WAI marketplace
- Join the corporate training program
- Access premium mentorship and support
