# Partnership Milestones — Redesigned for Incremental Progress

## Core Problem with Original Design

Old structure:
- Builder (300-800 pts): 500-point gap to next level
- User earns ~50 pts/month: Takes 10 months to unlock next level
- Result: Feels stuck, demotivating

**Solution:** Micro-milestones with incremental benefits every 50-100 points

## Achievability Analysis

### At Different Engagement Levels

**Casual User (1-2 hours/week)**
- 10-20 pts/week
- 40-80 pts/month
- Reaches Rooted (100 pts) in ~2 months
- Reaches Builder (300 pts) in ~6 months
- Milestone every 2-4 weeks

**Regular User (5-10 hours/week)**
- 30-50 pts/week
- 120-200 pts/month
- Reaches Rooted in ~3 weeks
- Reaches Builder in ~2 months
- Milestone every 1-2 weeks

**Power User (10+ hours/week)**
- 60-100 pts/week
- 240-400 pts/month
- Reaches Rooted in ~1 week
- Reaches Builder in ~3 weeks
- Milestone every 3-5 days

**What This Means:**
✅ Casual users: See progress every 2-4 weeks (feels good)
✅ Regular users: See progress every 1-2 weeks (momentum builds)
✅ Power users: Progress constantly visible (addictive in good way)

## The Psychological Win

### Sprint Model
Think of it like fitness:
- Can't finish a marathon mentally
- But a 5km sprint feels achievable
- Every sprint is a win
- Multiple wins = motivation to keep going

**Current system:** "I need 500 more points" (demotivating)
**New system:** "I need 15 more points for next milestone" (achievable) × 10 milestones (path clear)

### Milestone Celebration
Every 50 points = a thing worth celebrating
```
✨ You unlocked: Voice Badge
   You can now vote on feature requests!
```

vs. Generic point display:
```
250 / 800 pts to next level
```

## Why This Works

1. **Frequent Wins** — Milestone every 2-4 weeks (casual) to every few days (power user)
2. **Clear Path** — Know exactly what's 50 points away
3. **Psychological Momentum** — Multiple small wins > one big win
4. **Visible Progress** — Progress bar constantly moves
5. **Celebration** — Every unlock feels earned and special
6. **Flexible Pace** — Your activity level determines your tempo
7. **Always Something Next** — Never feel "stuck"

---

## Avoiding Burnout

Important to not make it feel like a chore:
- No daily quests or "streak punishments"
- No FOMO ("milestone expires!")
- No artificial gates ("come back tomorrow")
- Progress is purely based on real engagement
- Can take breaks without losing progress

**Philosophy:** Build momentum, not addiction. Show clear progress. Celebrate wins. That's it.
