# Partnership Level System — Full UI Design

## Core Philosophy
Every user action deepens their partnership stake in WAI. Not gamification—actual ownership. Your contributions matter because you're building this together.

---

## Partnership Levels & Milestones

### Level 1: **Seed** (0-100 points)
*You're planting something.*
- Created account
- Completed profile
- Joined first course or started creating
- Posted in community once

**Unlock:** Basic creator/student tools

### Level 2: **Rooted** (100-300 points)
*You're growing here.*
- Completed 2 courses OR published first course
- 5+ community posts/comments
- Made first earnings or first purchase
- Helped 1+ other member

**Unlock:** Community moderation tools, early access to features

### Level 3: **Builder** (300-800 points)
*You're shaping the foundation.*
- Published 3+ courses OR enrolled in 10+ courses
- $100+ earned/spent
- 50+ community contributions
- Mentored someone or received mentorship

**Unlock:** Creator fund access, featured creator status, community steering voice

### Level 4: **Steward** (800-2000 points)
*You're holding space for others.*
- 5+ published courses OR completed 20+ courses
- $500+ earned/spent
- 200+ community contributions
- Led workshop or community event
- Multiple mentorship relationships

**Unlock:** Revenue sharing committee voice, governance participation

### Level 5: **Elder** (2000+ points)
*You're shaping the future.*
- 10+ courses OR platform power user
- $2000+ impact (earned/spent)
- 500+ community contributions
- Built community initiatives
- Shaped policy/features through feedback

**Unlock:** Board advisory access, direct influence on platform direction, featured elder status

## Key Design Principles

1. **Celebration, Not Competition**
   - Show contributions, not rankings
   - Celebrate all types of partnership
   - No "losing" or "falling behind"

2. **Transparency**
   - Clear scoring visible
   - Show how to earn more points
   - No hidden algorithms

3. **Meaning Over Metrics**
   - Points unlock real privileges (governance voice)
   - Not just badges—actual influence
   - Your level = your stake in decisions

4. **Inclusive Contribution**
   - Different paths to same partnership level
   - Value creators, students, community builders equally
   - Small acts (forum help) count as much as big acts

5. **Visual Clarity**
   - Consistent icons for actions
   - Color-coded by contribution type
   - Progress bars show clear path forward
